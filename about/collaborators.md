# Collaborators

## IntAct and the IMEx Consortium

IntAct is a member of the [IMEx Consortium](https://www.imexconsortium.org), an international collaboration between a group of major public interaction data providers who have agreed to share curation effort and practices. 

## IMEx Consortium active members

* [DIP](http://dip.doe-mbi.ucla.edu/)
* [IID](http://ophid.utoronto.ca/)
* [InnateDB](http://www.innatedb.com/)
* [MINT](https://mint.bio.uniroma2.it/)
* [MatrixDB](http://matrixdb.univ-lyon1.fr/)
* [UniProt group](https://www.uniprot.org/)
* [Swiss-Prot group, SIB](https://www.expasy.org/resources/uniprotkb-swiss-prot)
